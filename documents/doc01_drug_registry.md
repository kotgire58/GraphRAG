# Drug Registry — Approved Pharmaceutical Compounds

## Metformin
- Generic name: Metformin hydrochloride
- Brand names: Glucophage, Fortamet, Glumetza
- Drug class: Biguanide
- FDA approval year: 1994
- Manufacturer: Bristol-Myers Squibb (original), now generic
- Primary manufacturer of generic supply: Teva Pharmaceuticals, Mylan Pharmaceuticals
- Dosage forms: Tablet 500mg, 850mg, 1000mg; Extended release tablet
- Route of administration: Oral
- Half-life: 4 to 8.7 hours
- Renal excretion: 90 percent excreted unchanged in urine
- Protein binding: Negligible
- Storage requirement: Room temperature, protect from moisture
